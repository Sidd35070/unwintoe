Welcome to UNWINTOE

UNWINTOE is an unbeatable tic-tac-toe game created using Python and Flask.

UNWINTOE stands for "Unwinnable Tic-tac-toe". No matter what your skill level at this game is, you can always try the game and see if you can win it (let me know if you do).

INSTRUCTIONS TO PLAY

1: Enter your name on the home page and click 'start game'

2: The computer starts the game and chooses randomly any of the nine boxes.

3: Choose any box and click "Play X here" to play your chance

4: Keep playing until a winner is declared or the match is draw.

5: Click "Play again" button to play the game again
